✦ Our Story

Grown by community.
Shared with love.

First Harvest provides livelihood to members of the Gawad Kalinga communities. It aims to give mothers a healthy alternative to feed their families and to increase the quality of agricultural produce — creating globally competitive products at par with, or even better than, imported brands.

First Harvest Adlai is grown from the highlands of North Cotabato and Bukidnon, supporting the farming communities from that area. Every jar, every bag, every bite is a story of dignity and hope.

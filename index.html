Our Story

Food Made From Community, For Community

First Harvest provides livelihood to the mothers and families of Gawad Kalinga communities across the Philippines. Every product you buy directly supports farming families.

We aim to give families a healthy, natural alternative — increasing the quality of agricultural produce to come up with globally competitive products that are at par, or even better than imported brands.

Our Adlai grains come from the highlands of North Cotabato and Bukidnon, supporting farming communities in those areas.

2. Products

All First Harvest products are handcrafted by community members. As natural, artisan products, slight variations in color, texture, or appearance may occur between batches. These are not defects.

  • All products are made without artificial preservatives.

🔄 Return & Refund Policy

Our Commitment

At First Harvest, we take pride in the quality of every product made by our Gawad Kalinga community. If something is not right, we want to make it right.

Eligible for Return or Replacement

  • Product arrived damaged, broken, or leaking
  • Wrong item was delivered
  • Product is spoiled or has an off smell upon opening (within shelf life)
  • Significant quantity shortage vs. labeled weight

Not Eligible for Return

  • Change of mind after delivery
  • Natural color or texture variation between batches (this is normal for artisan products)
  • Products opened and partially consumed
  • Issues reported more than 48 hours after delivery

How to Request a Return

Message us on WhatsApp at +63 977 603 3476 within 48 hours of receiving your order. Please include:

  • Your name and order reference
  • A clear photo of the issue
  • Brief description of the problem

Resolution

We will respond within 24 hours. Eligible claims will be resolved via replacement item or store credit. Cash refunds are processed within 5–7 business days via the original payment method.

Bundle Orders

For bundle and corporate orders, please contact us at firstharvest@gawadkalinga.com for a case-by-case resolution.
